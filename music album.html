American Teen

Kahlid is a 23 year old American singer and songwriter very popular in the R&B industry. He has 49.4 million monthly listeners on spotify along with 7.4 million followers on instagram. He started off as a highscooler who wrote songs as a hobby and one day decided to publish them onto a smaller music platform, soundcloud. His song Location and Young Dumb & Broke certifed as double platinum by the Recording Industry of America and was the start of his very sucessful carrer in the music industry.
